Generating Infinite Talking Videos with Your Words and Voice

We present MagicInfinite, a novel diffusion Transformer (DiT) framework that transcends traditional portrait animation limitations, enabling high-fidelity animation of diverse character types including realistic human portraits, full-body figures, and stylized anime characters. Our approach addresses core challenges through three key innovations. First, we employ 3D full-attention mechanisms that jointly model spatial and temporal dimensions, paired with a sliding window denoising strategy, ensuring robust temporal coherence and visual fidelity across infinite audio lengths and varied character styles, be it humans, animals, or animated figures. Second, we introduce a two-stage curriculum learning scheme that achieves unprecedented multi-modal control, integrating driving audio for precise lip synchronization, textual prompts for expressive dynamics and scene context, and reference images for identity preservation. This allows users to animate characters flexibly, directing their behavior through text and visuals over arbitrarily long sequences. Third, we integrate region-specific masks with adaptive loss functions to precisely regulate the influence of audio on key facial regions, resolving the competing influence problem between global textual control and local audio guidance while supporting speaker-specific animations in solo or multi-character setups. To boost efficiency, we apply comprehensive distillation techniques for sampling steps and classifier-free guidance, achieving a remarkable 20× acceleration in inference speed without compromising quality. With these upgrades, we can generate a 10-second 540x540p video in 10 seconds or a 720x720p video in 30 seconds on 8 H100 GPUs.

Diverse Facial And Body Orientations

MagicInfinite supports the animation of reference images featuring diverse facial and body orientations. This includes individuals facing forward, in profile, or even with near-backward postures.

Diverse Character Animations Scales

MagicInfinite supports character animations of varying scales, ranging from ultra-close-up portrait images that fully occupy the screen to half-body and full-body representations.

Diverse Character Styles

MagicInfinite supports a diverse range of character styles, including anime, cartoon, unique artistic styles, and even animals.

Anime And Cartoon Style

MagicInfinite enables the animation of a wide variety of cartoon characters and animals, generating dynamic, expressive, and naturally realistic anime-style videos.

Special artistic styles

MagicInfinite also supports portrait images of characters with unique artistic styles, accommodating various facial orientations and character scales, including close-up, half-body, and full-body representations.

Architecture Overview

MagicInfinite employs a hybrid dual-to-single-stream denoising network with Audio Cross-Attention in final blocks. MLLM encodes static portrait and text into tokens, concatenated for T2V, refined, and denoised. Wav2Vec encodes audio, resampled by an Audio Encoder, and guided by a Face Region Mask for precise lip sync and adaptive loss.
